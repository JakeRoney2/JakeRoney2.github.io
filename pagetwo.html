Why should you become a fan?

The difficult truth is that the Timberwolves are considered one of if not the worst franchise in NBA history. Here is a great example of this truth. BUT...That just means they have nothing to lose, which means it is a great time to become a fan. The Timberwolves are well on their way this season and in the future to become a well respected franchise and even considered a dynasty. Since they have signed multiple players during the offseason in the last few years and have had success when drafting recently, their future is looking very bright.

What about the Timberwolves mascot?


Crunch the Wolf is the Timberwolves mascot. He debuted in the 2003 season home opener against the Utah Jazz. Since then he was been a staple in Target Center and the Timberwolves community. He often makes appearances at various community and charity events around Minnesota.
