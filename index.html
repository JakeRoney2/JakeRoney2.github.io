THE MINNESOTA TIMBERWOLVES (and why you should become a fan)

First of all what are the Minnesota Timberwolves?

Well the Timberwolves are a professional basketball team that plays
in the NBA (National Basketball Association). They first joined the
NBA as an expansion team in the 1989-90 season along with the
Orlando Magic. They are a member of the Northwest Division in the
Western Conference.



To the left is the progression of the Timberwolves logos since their inaugural season in 1989. The first logo is from the 1989 season, which they kept until 1997 and switched to a scarier looking wolf. The Timberwolves did not switch their logo again until 2009 with just some minor colour changes, and then took another 8 years until 2017 when they unveiled their current logo.


The Timberwolves play in an arena that holds about 20,000 people called Target Center that is located in downtown Minneapolis. Just a short 12 hour drive away!


Recent History and Awards:

Sadly the Timberwolves have not won a championship but that might change this year...(Update: They did not win, lost in the Western Conference Finals). The closest they have gotten to a championship was in the 2003-04 season in which they got to the Western Conference Finals but sadly lost to the Los Angeles Lakers.

Since the Timberwolves are a fairly new franchise, they have only had one player win the Michael Jordan MVP award which was Kevin Garnett during the 2003-04 season. Anthony Edwards the current shooting guard for the Timberwolves has the best shot on the team to win it as he was even top 3 in voting at some points this season.

Some other notable awards won by Timberwolves players include:

Kevin Garnett - NBA All-Rookie Team Selection, 7 All-Defensive Team Selections, 7 All-NBA Selections, and 9 All-Star Selections.
Kevin Love - NBA All-Rookie Team Selection, George Mikan Most Improved Player Award, 2 All-NBA Selections, and 3 All-Star Selections
Andrew Wiggins - 3 Rookie Of The Month Selections, NBA All-Rookie Team Selection, and Wilt Chamberlain Rookie Of The Year Award
Karl Anthony-Towns - 2 All-NBA Selections, 9 Player Of The Week Selections, NBA All-Rookie Team Selection, Wilt Chamberlain Rookie Of The Year, and 4 All-Star Selections
Anthony Edwards - 3 Rookie Of The Month Selections, NBA All-Rookie Team Selection, 1 Player Of The Week Selection, 1 All-NBA selection, and 2 All-Star Selections
