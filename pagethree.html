Community Work

Are the Timberwolves involved in the community?

The Timberwolves are very involved in the community in Minneapolis. During the 2022-2023 season, the Timberwolves and their WNBA team the Minnesota Lynx donated more than $1.5 million, had more than 580 volunteering hours, served 520 non profits, gave away more than 26,000 tickets, and impacted more than 43,000 youth and families.


    The Timberwolves and their partner franchises have their own nonprofit called the Fastbreak Foundation, which aims to have great outcomes in education, inclusion, and wellness. The campaign has a “Drive to Five” goal of accomplishing the following over the next five years:

  1. $5 million dollars distributed via monetary & in-kind donations

  2. 500,000 Minnesota youth & families impacted

  3. 15,000 Volunteer Hours

  4. 2,500 Non-profits served

  5. 500 Player Appearances
